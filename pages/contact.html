Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical web development project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex applications can take 2-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

What is included in your web development services?

Our web development services include responsive design, custom development, SEO optimization, performance optimization, security implementation, content management system integration, and 3 months of free maintenance and support.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes! We offer comprehensive maintenance packages that include regular backups, security updates, performance monitoring, content updates, and technical support. Our maintenance plans start at $99/month and can be customized to your needs.

What are your payment terms and pricing structure?

We typically require 50% upfront and 50% upon completion for projects. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. All our quotes are detailed and transparent with no hidden fees. Payment can be made via bank transfer, PayPal, or cryptocurrency.

Can you work with existing websites or do you only build new ones?

We work with both existing websites and create new ones from scratch. Whether you need a complete redesign, functionality improvements, performance optimization, or just minor updates, we can help modernize and enhance your current website.

Do you offer digital marketing services?

Absolutely! Our digital marketing services include SEO, PPC advertising, social media management, email marketing, content creation, and analytics. We create comprehensive marketing strategies tailored to your business goals and target audience.
